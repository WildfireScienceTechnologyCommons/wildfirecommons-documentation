# About FireForge

[FireForge](https://fireforge.wildfirecommons.org/) is a community platform designed to accelerate the development of wildfire science workflows, foster collaboration, and share resources for innovative solutions to wildfires.


With FireForge, you can:

- **Discover** datasets, models, and services through the [Community Catalog](https://fireforge.wildfirecommons.org/ckandata).

<img src="../pics/community-catalog.png" style="border: 2px solid black;">

- **Contribute** by [registering](../add-resources/register-data.md) your own data, models, or services to the Commons.

<img src="../pics/register.png" style="border: 2px solid black;">

- **Build** interactive workspaces to showcase data, develop models, or design end-to-end workflows.

<img src="../pics/workspace.png" style="border: 2px solid black;">

- **Collaborate** by [creating projects](../start/working-tutorial.md#creating-your-first-project) that bring together researchers, students, and practitioners.

<img src="../pics/project.png" style="border: 2px solid black;">

- **Engage** the community by [hosting Data Challenges](../data-challenges/hosting-a-data-challenge.md) that inspire new approaches to wildfire problems.

<img src="../pics/data-challenge.png" style="border: 2px solid black;">
