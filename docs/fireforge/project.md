# WSTC Projects

Projects are collaborative spaces where your research group and partners can build and share collective workflows.

<img src="../pics/first-project.png" style="border: 2px solid black;">

With Projects, you can:

- Invite members to join and collaborate

- Share and manage workspaces with your team

- Allow any member to create new workspaces

- Integrate curated catalogs directly into a workspace

- Access a shared persistent folder within JupyterHub

To set up your first project, follow the [working tutorial](../start/working-tutorial.md#creating-your-first-project).
